About

I’m a first year PhD student at the University of Oxford, working with Michael Bronstein, Yari Gal and Haggai Maron. I’m also affiliated with the AIMS CDT program.

I’m interested in the the symmetry and structure of neural network parameter spaces and in learning with symmetries.
